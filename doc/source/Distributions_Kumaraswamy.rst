Kumaraswamy distribution
========================

The Kumaraswamy distribution is a univariate density on the interval
[0,B] with the p.d.f.

.. math::

   p(x) =  \frac{ab}{B^{ab}} x^{a-1} (B^a-x^a)^{b-1}

:math:`a>0` and :math:`b>0` are shape parameters of the distribution.
